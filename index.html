A stylized top down beat 'em up based around punching literally anything that gets in your way.




About

Boss Breaker is a twin-stick beat 'em up set within the Nexus Orion Labs corporate compound. You were hired as a product tester for the all-new "Breaker Fists," but after a chance encounter and a paperwork mixup, you'll have to unravel the secrets of your employer and take them down once and for all.

Any dissent is immediately silenced, and anyone caught spreading it is never seen again. The entire company is against you, so do you have what it takes to take it down without being annihilated first?

Designed to be picked up and played right away, Boss Breaker allows you to ignore the story completely if you want, instead opting to focus on destroying everything in your path. If that's not your style, stick around and uncover the secret history of Nexus Orion Labs. No matter how you play, we have you covered.
